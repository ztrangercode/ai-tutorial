### 3. Open on your device

1.  Download the **Expo Go** app on your phone (iOS or Android).
2.  Scan the QR code displayed in your terminal.
3.  The app will load and connect to your local inference server.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Network Connection**: Ensure your phone and the computer running the server are on the same Wi-Fi network.
